About Us

Experience excellence in engineering education at Sri Venkateswara College.

Our Story

Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering (SVCE) is a leading institute located in Pennalur, Sriperumbudur, near Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Established in 1985 by the Southern Petrochemical Industries Corporation (SPIC) group, SVCE is recognized among the top engineering colleges of Anna University in Tamil Nadu. It holds the distinction of being a Tier-I institution among self-financing colleges.

Mission

Our mission at SVCE is to evolve into a "CENTRE OF EXCELLENCE" for engineering education at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels. We aim to nurture the holistic development of individuals, emphasizing ethical values, and preparing them to meet the evolving challenges of the industry and diverse societal needs.

Vision

Our vision is to lead in higher technical education and research. We strive to provide state-of-the-art facilities that transform learners into global contributors and achievers.
